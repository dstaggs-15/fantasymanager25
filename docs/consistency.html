How to Use This Tool

This page helps you understand player volatility, which is crucial for making tough start/sit decisions.

  • Mean PPG: A player's average fantasy points per game. Good for a baseline, but doesn't tell the whole story.
  • Std Dev (Standard Deviation): Measures how much a player's score varies from their average. A lower number is better, indicating a more consistent, reliable player.
  • Ceiling PPG: The player's 90th percentile score. This is their "boom" potential—the kind of score that can win you a week.
  • Floor PPG: The player's 10th percentile score. This is their "bust" potential—the score that can lose you a week.
  • Consistency %: The percentage of games where the player scored above a useful baseline for their position (e.g., 10+ points for a RB). Use this to choose between players with similar averages.
